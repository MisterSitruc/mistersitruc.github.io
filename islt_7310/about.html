About Me

My name is Curtis Thomas. I’m a middle school librarian in Springfield, MO. My wife teaches 3rd grade. We have two sons, a three year old and a nine month old. In my free time I enjoy reading science fiction and fantasy (or just about anything else I can get my hands on). I also like playing video games and spending time with my family.
I was a high school English teacher before becoming a school librarian. I taught anything from core English courses to film and broadcast to running the yearbook. I also spent time as a college teacher while earning my Master’s degree in English. Now I get to combine my passion for reading with my love of technology, working with teachers and students to learn, research, and create. One of my favorite parts of my job is helping a reluctant reader find a book they love. I also love trying new tech tools and sharing them with teachers.

• Education Overview • Hillcrest High School, Springfield, MO –– 2009 • Missouri State University, Springfield, MO — Bachelor of Science in Education, 2012 • University of Missouri, Columbia, MO — Master of Arts in English, 2016 • University of Missouri, Columbia, MO — Educational Specialist in Learning Technologies and Design with an Emphasis in Technology in Schools, ongoing • Missouri Teaching Certifications • English Education, grades 9-12 • Library Media Specialist, grades K-12
